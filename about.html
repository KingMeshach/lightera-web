Our Mission

LightEra International Admissions & Scholarships was created to help ambitious students secure university admission and full funding across the globe. We handle all the stress, heavy lifting, and confusing application portals so that you can focus entirely on your future.

Our Service Packages & Fees

1. Full Application-to-Visa Package

If an applicant wants LightEra to oversee their entire journey from start to finish—from searching and applying to the university all the way to handling their visa processing—there is a set fee for this complete service.

2. Custom School or Scholarship Package

If an applicant already has specific universities or specific scholarships they want to target, and they want LightEra to manage those specific applications for them, we have a dedicated fee for that.

3. Visa-Only Processing Package

If an applicant has already successfully gotten admission on their own, but wants LightEra to handle and process their visa and embassy preparation, we have a specific fee for that.

4. International Exam Registration

We take care of direct official registrations for IELTS and other international exams to guarantee your test slots are safely locked in.

Our Absolute Tracking Promise

Our precise tracking process enables applicants to see exactly what stage we are currently in for every single school or scholarship they asked us to apply for, or that we are actively applying for on their behalf. We maintain total stage visibility so you are never left guessing.
